#### Overview

After working closer with Android developers I saw the lack of a proper unit test tool.
I gave and idea of porting Spock framework, that we prefer to use in JVM apps,
to Android. Thanks to support of Przemek Jakubczyk we made it happen.

Built on top of Spock and Robolectric - RoboSpock gives a great support
for Android developers, who value TDD.
