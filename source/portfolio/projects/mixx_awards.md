#### Overview

For MIXX Awards contest system I was a one-man-army.
I was gathering requirement and use cases from the client,
as well as planning and developing the whole application.

After a great cooperation on the first edition I had a pleasure to maintain the app for next 3 years.
This project gave me priceless knowledge on how great a good cooperation
with the stakeholder might be. I also learned the value of continuous value delivery and constant feedback.
