#### Overview

Working for a huge pharmaceutical corporation has it's ups and downs.
One of the biggest disadvantages for me was the age and complexity of the application.
I had a chance to understand the meaning of the saying:
*Data ages like wine, whereas application like fish*.

The record-holder was an 11 years old app, which features no one was able to fully describe.
Another problem is that getting change requests through several layers of management
often have no value left for the end user.

While working with that application I learned mostly how not to make software.
To me it will always be a warning against throwing features into a product the easiest possible way.

The best part of that project was that the team adopted Kanban,
which helped going down with average lead time from months to weeks.
